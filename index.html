Tech Skills Training Workshop

Join our free training sessions designed for beginners and aspiring tech professionals. Learn essential skills, meet like-minded learners, and grow your digital confidence.

Courses we offer include:

  1. Basic Computer Skills
  2. Microsoft Office Tools
  3. Web Development(HTML, CSS, JavaScript)
  4. Intro to AI tools
  5. Internet Safety

Learning Goals include:

  • Build confidence in using computers and digital tools.
  • Learn essential tech skills for education, career, or personal growth.
  • Gain hands-on experience in creating and managing content.
  • Understand the structure of a website and how to build one.
  • Improve digital literacy and become more independent in tech use.
  • Prepare for further learning in programming or IT-related fields.
